Which format do you need?

EquitableDocs serves two distinct groups of students with disabilities. Tell us which track applies when you upload — we handle the rest.

🔊

Screen reader accessible

We add proper heading structure, reading order, alt text for images, and table markup. Output is a tagged PDF conforming to PDF/UA — compatible with JAWS, NVDA, VoiceOver, and braille displays.

For students who are blind or have low vision
📖

Easy Read

We rewrite complex content using plain language, short sentences, and visual supports — following international Easy Read guidelines. Output is a document that can be understood without assistance.

For students with learning disabilities or intellectual disabilities
How it works

Three steps to an accessible document

No account required. Upload your document and we handle the rest.

1

Upload your document

Upload a PDF, Word file, or PowerPoint. Scanned documents and images work too. Tell us the language, which format you need — screen reader accessible or Easy Read — and any context that will help our remediators.

PDF · Word · PowerPoint · Scanned images
2

We make it accessible

Our tools add proper headings, alt text for images, reading order, and table structure — or rewrite the content in Easy Read format. Trained Guild Members review every document for quality before delivery.

Guild Member reviewed · veraPDF validated
3

Download or receive by email

Get your accessible document by email or WhatsApp. Every document comes with a tracking ID so you can check its status at any time.

Email · WhatsApp · Download
Who we serve

Built for students and institutions in the Global South.

EquitableDocs provides document remediation for students with disabilities and builds in-house accessibility capacity at higher education institutions — supported by trained Guild Members.

For universities

Cost-recovery pricing · No profit margin

We install a document accessibility pipeline inside your institution, train your staff to run it, and hand it over. You pay only the infrastructure costs — nothing more. Our measure of success is how quickly you no longer need us. Most institutions reach full independence within 12 to 18 months.
