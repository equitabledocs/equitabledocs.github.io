How it works

Three steps to an accessible document

No software to install. No account required. Upload your document and we handle the rest.

1

Upload your document

Upload a PDF, Word file, or PowerPoint. Scanned documents and images work too. Tell us which language and what kind of output you need.

2

We make it accessible

Our tools add proper headings, alt text for images, reading order, and table structure. Trained Guild Members review the result for quality.

3

Download or receive by email

Get your accessible document by email or WhatsApp. Every document comes with a tracking ID so you can check its status at any time.
