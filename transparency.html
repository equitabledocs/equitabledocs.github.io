Cost structure

What university partners pay for

Students with print disabilities never pay. University pricing is cost-recovery only: actual infrastructure costs plus a 10% sustainability buffer. No profit margin. No hidden fees.

Indicative pricing for university partnerships (INR, as of March 2026)
Document type Specification Indicative price What drives the cost
Standard document 1-20 pages, native PDF Rs 2-4 per page Server compute + volunteer review time
Standard document 1-20 pages, scanned (OCR required) Rs 4-7 per page Adds OCR processing + quality review
STEM document Any length, equations present Rs 8-15 per page Equation extraction API + STEM specialist time
Complex layout Multi-column, heavy tables, forms Rs 6-10 per page Additional reading order + table markup time
Bulk batch (50+ pages) Standard documents Rs 1.5-3 per page Volume discount on server and queue costs
Bulk batch (50+ pages) STEM documents Rs 6-10 per page Volume discount on tooling; STEM specialist time constant
Document Format Template One-time build per document family Rs 800-1,500 Template creation + testing on 5 sample files
Annual training session Full day, up to 15 staff Rs 8,000-12,000 Trainer fee (paid to Guild Trainer) + platform cost

Prices are denominated in INR for Indian institutions. International pricing on application. These rates are reviewed quarterly.

Methodology

How we calculate costs

Every component of our pricing is derived from actual operating costs. Here is what each line item covers.

Server compute

Time on our VPS or cloud infrastructure per document page. Calculated from actual processing logs. Infrastructure runs on Docker containers hosted on Linux VPS.

Tooling costs

Software licence costs amortised per page. Equation extraction API cost per equation for STEM content. Open-source tools (Tesseract, veraPDF) carry zero licence cost.

Guild Member time

Estimated at Rs 150 per hour fair value. Minutes per task type published openly. Guild Members are skilled practitioners contributing their expertise — not underpaid labour.

Platform overhead

Hosting, domain, backups, monitoring, email delivery. Fixed costs divided by documents processed per month.

Sustainability buffer

10% above total cost. Funds development of new features, the training programme, and Guild infrastructure. This is the full margin — there is no additional profit.

What is NOT included

There is no sales margin, no investor return, no executive compensation. EquitableDocs is a mission-driven initiative, not a commercial business. Every rupee is accounted for.

Accountability

Our transparency commitments

These are binding commitments, not aspirations. If we fail to meet them, we expect to be held accountable.

  • ✓ Free for students with print disabilities — always. No fees, no freemium, no upselling. Students with print disabilities at higher education institutions in the Global South will never be charged for document remediation.
  • ✓ Cost-recovery pricing only for universities. University partners pay actual infrastructure costs plus a 10% sustainability buffer. No profit margin. The full cost breakdown is published on this page.
  • ✓ Quarterly cost updates. This page is updated every quarter with current pricing components, any changes to rates, and an explanation of what changed and why.
  • ✓ Open progress reporting. Year 1 targets and actual progress are published on this page. We do not hide behind vague language when targets are missed — we explain what happened and what we are doing about it.
  • ✓ Guild Members are fairly valued. Guild Member time is valued at Rs 150 per hour — a fair rate, not an exploitative one. This rate is published and reviewed annually.
  • ✓ No data monetisation. Documents uploaded to EquitableDocs are never used for training AI models, sold to third parties, or shared beyond the remediation process. Our privacy policy details exactly what happens to every document.
  • ✓ Independence is the goal. University partnerships are designed to end. Our measure of success is how quickly an institution no longer needs us. Most should reach full independence within 12 to 18 months.

We are happy to walk through every line item. Transparency is not a marketing claim — it is how we operate.
