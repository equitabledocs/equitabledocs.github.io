Submit your document. We make it accessible.

Three simple steps

How it works

1

Email us your document

Send your document to submit@equitabledocs.org. Tell us your name, what language the document is in, and what format you need (accessible PDF, Word, or EPUB).

2

We make it accessible

Our team of trained volunteers and automated tools add proper headings, image descriptions, reading order, and table structure. Every document is validated against international accessibility standards.

3

We send it back

You receive your accessible document by email within 5-7 working days. A compliance certificate comes with every document.

Submit your document

How to submit your document

Email your document to:

submit@equitabledocs.org

In your email, please include:

  • Your full name
  • The language of the document (for example: English, Hindi, Tamil)
  • What format you need: Accessible PDF, Accessible Word, or Accessible EPUB
  • Any specific needs (for example: equations need MathML, tables need headers)

We accept: PDF, Word (.doc, .docx), PowerPoint (.ppt, .pptx), and scanned documents (images or scanned PDFs).

We will reply within 48 hours with a tracking ID. Your accessible document will be ready within 5-7 working days.

A fully accessible upload portal is coming soon. For now, email is the simplest and most accessible way to submit your document.
Common questions

Frequently asked questions

Is this really free?+
Yes. EquitableDocs is a non-profit initiative. Students with disabilities never pay. Ever.
What documents can I submit?+
PDF, Word, PowerPoint, scanned pages, and images of documents. Any language. We support 100+ languages through our OCR pipeline.
How long does it take?+
Standard documents: 5-7 working days. STEM documents with equations: up to 10 working days. We will give you a tracking ID so you can check the status.
